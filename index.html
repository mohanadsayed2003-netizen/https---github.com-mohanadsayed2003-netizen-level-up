About levelup

levelup was founded in 2025 with a simple mission: to make learning fun for children aged 5-12. Our team of educators and game designers work together to create engaging content that aligns with school curricula while keeping kids entertained.

We believe that every child learns differently, which is why we offer multiple ways to explore each topic through games, stories, and interactive challenges.
